Your First Session

What to Expect

At your initial assessment, your physiotherapist will visit your home and take time to understand your condition, history, and goals. You'll work together to develop a treatment plan tailored to your specific needs and lifestyle.

Each session typically lasts 45–60 minutes. Just have a comfortable space where you can move freely — we'll bring any necessary equipment.

Common Questions

Do I need a referral?

No — you can contact us directly. We also accept referrals from GPs, specialists, and other healthcare providers.

What do I need to prepare?

Just a comfortable space where you can move freely. We'll bring any necessary equipment. Wear loose, comfortable clothing.

How many sessions will I need?

This depends on your condition. Your physiotherapist will give an honest estimate at your initial assessment.

Is my condition covered?

Our services are funded privately or through healthcare schemes. Contact us for details about your specific situation.
