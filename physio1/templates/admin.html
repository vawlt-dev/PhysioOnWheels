System

Settings

Online Payment

Controls whether the credit card form is active on the booking page. When disabled, the card fields are greyed out and non-interactive — patients can still submit a booking request, and you'll contact them to arrange payment separately.

Credit card payment   {% if c.settings.payment_enabled %}ENABLED{% else %}DISABLED{% endif %}
When off: card fields are greyed out. Button reads "Request Booking" instead of "Pay".
Note: This is a demo payment system. No real charges are made. When payment is enabled, bookings are marked as "Paid" automatically. When disabled, bookings are marked as "Pending" and you arrange payment directly with the patient.

Online Booking

Controls whether the booking form is accessible. When disabled, patients visiting /booking see a message directing them to call or email instead.

Online booking   {% if c.settings.booking_enabled %}ENABLED{% else %}DISABLED{% endif %}
When off: the booking page shows a "call us" message instead of the form.
