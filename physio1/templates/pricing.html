Funding & Subsidies

Payment & Funding Options

Private Payment

We accept payment by credit card, bank transfer, or cash. Payment is due at the time of your session. Online booking requires a card to hold your appointment.

Healthcare Funding

Our services may be funded through ACC, health insurance, or other healthcare schemes depending on your condition. Contact us before booking to discuss your options — we're happy to help you understand what funding may be available to you.

Cancellation Policy

We ask for at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule a session. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a fee. We understand that circumstances change — please contact us as soon as possible if you need to adjust your booking.
